Welcome to Spicul auriu

"Spicul Auriu" invites you on a culinary journey through the heart of Moldova. Our restaurant captures the essence of Moldovan traditions, serving authentic dishes that showcase the rich flavors and heritage of the region.

With warm hospitality and a rustic ambiance, "Spicul Auriu" is the perfect place to savor time-honored Moldovan cuisine. Come experience a taste of Moldova in every delightful bite.
